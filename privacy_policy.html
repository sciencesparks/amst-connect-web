Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AMST Connect. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data is handled securely. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use the AMST Connect Android application.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our matchmaking services, we collect the following user data:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, marital status, and physical characteristics (e.g., height).
  • Sensitive Information: Religious background (Maslak) and family details, strictly provided by you for matchmaking purposes.
  • Media and Files: Photos uploaded directly by you for your profile or our public Success Gallery. We only access images you explicitly choose to upload.
  • App Activity: Interaction with other profiles (e.g., sending/receiving interests) and app usage patterns.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely to:

  • Create and manage your matchmaking account.
  • Display your profile to potential matches within the app.
  • Facilitate communication and "interests" between users.
  • Process premium subscription upgrades securely.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. We use trusted third-party services to operate our app securely. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase: Used for secure database storage, photo hosting, and user authentication.
  • Google Play Services & Google Pay: Used for processing secure in-app payments and subscriptions. We do not store your credit card or UPI details on our servers.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as your account is active. You have the right to delete your data at any time.

How to delete your account and data:

  • Inside the App: Navigate to My Profile, scroll to the bottom, and tap the red "Delete My Account" button in the Danger Zone. This will permanently erase your profile, photos, and match history from our servers.
  • Via Email: You may also request account deletion by emailing us directly at the contact address below from your registered email address.

6. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. We use secure databases (Supabase) with encrypted passwords and row-level security policies.

7. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 18. If we discover that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or data deletion requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@amstconnect.com
